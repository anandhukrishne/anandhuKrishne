Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to anandhukrishne.tech. This Privacy Policy outlines how information is handled when you visit this personal portfolio website. The primary purpose of this site is to showcase software development projects, including Java applications, UI prototypes, and academic research, for informational and educational purposes.

2. Information Collection

This website is designed to be lightweight and privacy-respecting. Data collection is kept to a minimum:

  • Voluntarily Provided Data: If you choose to reach out via a "Contact" form or direct email, standard communication data (such as your name, email address, and message content) will be collected.
  • Automatically Collected Data: Depending on the hosting platform (such as GitHub Pages) and any integrated analytics, standard web traffic data may be logged automatically. This can include your IP address, browser type, operating system, and the timestamps of your visit.

3. Use of Information

Any data collected is used strictly for the following operational purposes:

  • To respond to your direct inquiries, feedback, or collaboration requests regarding the showcased projects.
  • To monitor basic website performance, ensure site stability, and understand general visitor engagement.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

This site may utilize basic, essential cookies necessary for the website to function properly. If third-party tools (like simple traffic analytics or embedded media from other platforms) are utilized, those services may deploy their own cookies in accordance with their respective privacy policies. You can choose to disable cookies through your web browser settings.

5. Data Sharing and Security

Your personal information is treated with respect. It is never sold, traded, or rented to external parties. Information is only shared if required by law or to protect the technical integrity of the website. Reasonable digital security practices are maintained to protect any data routed through the site's contact channels.

6. External Links

This portfolio frequently links to external websites, such as open-source code repositories, educational platforms, or third-party project dependencies. Once you click a link and leave anandhukrishne.tech, this Privacy Policy no longer applies. You are encouraged to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

7. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically as new projects, features, or integrations are added to the portfolio. The "Effective Date" at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revisions.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact details provided directly on the website.
